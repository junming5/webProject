About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an addictive roguelike survival game where your objective is to survive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves of monsters. As you play, you'll automatically attack nearby enemies with your equipped weapons while focusing on movement and positioning to avoid damage.

The core gameplay loop involves defeating monsters, collecting experience gems, leveling up, and choosing new weapons or upgrades to become more powerful. Each run offers a unique experience with random encounters and upgrade options, encouraging multiple playthroughs to discover optimal strategies.

With its pixel art graphics, dynamic sound effects, and satisfying progression system, Monster Survivors creates an engaging experience that's easy to learn but difficult to master. How long can you survive against the monster apocalypse?

How to Play

Controls

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Your character attacks automatically when enemies are in range
  • Press ESC to pause the game
  • Use number keys 1-9 to activate special abilities (when available)

Gameplay Tips

  • Prioritize movement speed upgrades early for better survivability
  • Focus on a few complementary weapons rather than many different ones
  • Collect experience gems quickly before they disappear
  • Use terrain and obstacles to funnel enemies and create chokepoints
  • Don't stand still - keep moving to avoid being surrounded

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Monster Survivors free to play?

Yes, Monster Survivors is completely free to play right in your browser!

Can I save my progress?

The game automatically saves your high scores and unlocked characters when playing on the same device and browser.

How do I unlock new characters?

New characters are unlocked by reaching specific milestones, such as surviving for a certain amount of time or defeating special boss monsters.

Does the game work on mobile devices?

Yes, Monster Survivors is optimized for both desktop and mobile play with touch controls automatically enabled on mobile devices.
